    We are the Upsilon Chapter of Psi Upsilon at the University of Rochester. With a diverse group of members involved in highly demanding areas of study, we have consistanly been ranked among the top Greek organizations at our university.

  • Intellectual, moral, and social excellence.

    These are the qualities that, as members of the Upsilon Chapter, we aspire to uphold each day. We challenge ourselves to achieve successes inside and ouside of the classroom as well as in the Rochester community and our university.

  • Academic success. Service to society. Lifelong friendship.

    Our chapter has achieved numerous university and national awards for academics, service, and philanthropy. While achieving such recognition, we continued to make memories that will last a lifetime.

More About Us

What we're all about

Our fraternity seeks to inspire success, leadership, and respect in all of our members through the various activities we participate in. We pride ourselves on recruiting some of the finest men at the University of Rochester and growing together as valuable members of the community and as brothers.

Service

An important part of our success is giving back to the community. Our Philanthropy Committee is constantly searching for service opportunities around our university and in the Rochester community. We have recently averaged a total of 200-250 hours of community service per semester.

Convention

Our fraternity holds an annual convention each summer, bringing together delegates from all of our nearly 30 active chapters, our National Executive Council, and the Psi Upsilon Foundation. This gathering has been held all over the country from Lexington, KY to Seattle, WA and even here in Rochester, NY during the summer of 2018.
